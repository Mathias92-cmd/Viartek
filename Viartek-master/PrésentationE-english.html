How did the adventure begin?

Our history

Viartek started with the passionate idea of mixing art and technology. From our humble beginnings, we have grown to become a dynamic platform which organizes each year special events focused on computational creativity. Our history is marked by our commitment to include all the world, whatever the age or level. Over the years, we celebrated many successes and built a thriving community. Join us on this continuing adventure where art, technology and passion come together to create exceptional experiences.

Our values

At Viartek, our core values guide our business. We believe in inclusion, which means that everyone is welcome, regardless of age or skill level. skill. Creativity, visual art and technology are at the heart of what we do. We encourage continuous learning and collaboration. Our annual events

Every year, Viartek organizes special events where everything everyone from beginners to experts can show off their creativity in programming. For only €20, each participant can enjoy a complete experience with all the equipment provided on the spot. There are rewards to be won, but above all, it is an opportunity to discover or deepen your passion for creativity and programming. Join us for these moments exceptional events where we celebrate innovation and learn together.
